Introducing Photography Week

Welcome to the blog of Photography Week, the tablet-only photography magazine, which launches in mid-September! Photography Week will be a brand new experience for photographers its completely designed for tablet devices, such as the Apple iPad, from the ground up and will be packed with exciting interactive features, such as videos and image galleries. As the name suggests, itll come out every seven days.

Photography Week will also include all the great features youve come to know and love from photography magazines, such as in-depth, practical advice for getting more out of your camera and improving your photography, detailed reviews of the latest gear and interviews with top pros from around the world. But the crucial difference with Photography Week its that its a genuinely social photography magazine for todays photographer. We want it to be all about you and the adventures you have in photography, so were inviting you to get involved in every way. Not only do we want you to submit your photos for publication in our gallery, which you can do by uploading them to our Facebook page, but well also hold regular photo contests and invite you to share your opinions on important issues. We want you to feel that its your magazine above anything else.

All of which brings us to this here Tumblr blog. Designed to complement the magazine, the blogs mission is to bring you a dose of daily photographic inspiration. Well showcase a stunning photo from a hugely talented photographer every day of the week and tell you why we think its special. Please feel free to comment and share what you see with fellow photographers and help us celebrate the very best photography there is.

Well be making more exciting announcements about Photography Week, including when you can get it, in the coming weeks. Its going to be great and we really cant wait to share it with you, so check back regularly for updates and the odd surprise.

Thats it for now make sure you visit every day for your dose of photographic inspiration.
